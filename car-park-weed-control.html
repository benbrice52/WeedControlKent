What We Treat

We provide comprehensive weed control for all car park areas:

Treatment Frequency & Annual Programs

Regular maintenance provides the best results and value. We recommend:

Quarterly Treatments (Most Popular)

Four treatments per year (every 12-14 weeks) provide year-round weed control:

  • March/April: Pre-emptive treatment before spring growth surge
  • June/July: Peak growing season maintenance
  • September/October: Control of autumn regrowth
  • December/January: Winter treatment for early control

Best for: Retail parks, customer-facing businesses, high-visibility sites requiring consistently professional appearance.

Bi-Annual Treatments

Two treatments per year for lower-traffic or budget-conscious sites:

  • April/May: Spring treatment
  • August/September: Summer/autumn treatment

Best for: Staff car parks, industrial sites, moderate-use facilities.

Annual Treatment

Single comprehensive treatment, typically in April/May.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you work while the car park is in use?

Yes - we regularly treat operational car parks. We use temporary signage to guide customers around treated areas and most herbicides dry within 2-4 hours. For very busy sites, we can work section-by-section or schedule treatments during quieter periods (early mornings, evenings, or weekends).

How quickly do weeds die after treatment?

Visible yellowing typically starts within 7-14 days, with complete die-off within 3-4 weeks. Residual herbicides provide 8-16 weeks of protection against new growth, making quarterly treatments ideal for continuous control.

Will treatment damage line markings or tarmac?

No - we use herbicides specifically designed for hard surfaces that won't damage tarmac, block paving, or line markings. Our operators are trained to apply treatments carefully around markings and parking bay lines.

Do you need access to water on site?

No - we're completely self-sufficient with our own water supply. This makes scheduling easier and means we can treat sites without water access or tap availability.

What documentation do you provide?

We provide comprehensive health & safety documentation including risk assessments, method statements, COSHH assessments, insurance certificates, and operator qualification certificates. All documentation is provided before work commences.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We can typically schedule treatments within 3-5 working days for standard requests. Emergency callouts can often be accommodated next-day subject to weather and availability. Annual contract clients receive priority scheduling.
