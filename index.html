Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What types of properties do you treat? +
We specialise in commercial, industrial, and public sector properties including car parks, business premises, schools, industrial estates, and retail parks.
Are your treatments safe for children and pets? +
Yes, we use professional herbicides that are safe when applied correctly by our qualified operators. We provide clear guidance on re-entry times and offer specific treatments for sensitive environments.
How long do the results last? +
Results vary depending on treatment type. Typical treatments provide control for 3-6 months. We also offer residual treatments that prevent regrowth for up to 12 months.
Can you work outside normal business hours? +
Absolutely. We regularly work early mornings, evenings, and weekends to minimise disruption to your operations.
Do you offer contract services? +
Yes, we provide flexible maintenance contracts with scheduled treatments throughout the year for consistent weed control and reduced long-term costs.
