FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our weed control services

What types of properties do you treat? +
We specialise in commercial, industrial, and public sector properties including car parks, business premises, schools, industrial estates, retail parks, and municipal areas throughout Kent and South East London.
Are your treatments safe for children and pets? +
Yes, we use professional herbicides that are safe when applied correctly by our qualified operators. We provide clear guidance on re-entry times and offer specific treatments for sensitive environments like schools and public spaces.
How long do the results last? +
Results vary depending on treatment type and environmental conditions. Typical treatments provide effective control for 3-6 months. We also offer residual treatments that prevent regrowth for up to 12 months.
Can you work outside normal business hours? +
Absolutely. We regularly work early mornings, evenings, and weekends to minimise disruption to your business operations. Flexible scheduling is available for all our commercial clients.
Do you offer contract services? +
Yes, we provide flexible maintenance contracts with scheduled treatments throughout the year for consistent weed control, reduced long-term costs, and priority service scheduling.
What areas do you cover? +
We provide comprehensive coverage across all of Kent including Chatham, Maidstone, Canterbury, Gravesend, Dartford, Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, and South East London areas.
