Japanese Knotweed Removal

Japanese Knotweed is one of the UK's most invasive plants and can cause serious damage to properties, foundations, and drainage systems. Our specialist team provides comprehensive eradication services.

Our Japanese Knotweed Services Include:

  • Professional surveys and identification
  • Detailed treatment plans and quotations
  • Herbicide treatment programs (3-5 years typical)
  • Excavation and removal when required
  • Management plans for property transactions
  • Regular monitoring and follow-up treatments
  • Comprehensive documentation for mortgage lenders

Why choose us for Japanese Knotweed? We understand the legal implications and property concerns associated with Japanese Knotweed. Our BALI accreditation and professional certifications ensure you receive expert, compliant treatment that satisfies mortgage lender requirements.

  • Aquatic plant identification
  • Environmentally safe treatments
  • Compliance with water authority regulations

Expert Aquatic Management: Our PA6 AW certification demonstrates our expertise in working safely near water. We understand the environmental sensitivities and legal requirements for aquatic weed control.
