What is BALI Accreditation?

The British Association of Landscape Industries (BALI) is the UK's leading trade association for landscape professionals. BALI Accreditation is awarded only to companies that meet rigorous standards across all aspects of their business operations.

Roundwood Solutions achieved BALI Accreditation in October 2025, demonstrating our commitment to professional excellence. This accreditation means:

  • Quality Standards: We meet industry-leading standards for workmanship and service delivery
  • Professional Training: Our team holds recognized qualifications and receives ongoing professional development
  • Health & Safety: We maintain comprehensive health & safety policies and procedures
  • Insurance & Compliance: We carry appropriate insurance and comply with all relevant legislation
  • Business Integrity: We demonstrate financial stability and ethical business practices
  • Customer Focus: We follow industry best practices for customer service and communication

As a BALI-accredited company, we're regularly assessed to ensure we maintain these high standards. This gives our clients confidence that they're working with a professional, reliable service provider.
