What is BALI Accreditation?

The British Association of Landscape Industries (BALI) is the UK's leading trade association for professional landscaping and grounds maintenance companies. BALI accreditation is the industry's gold standard, demonstrating our commitment to excellence.

As a BALI Accredited member, Weed Control Kent has met rigorous standards in professional practice, business management, and service delivery. This gives our commercial clients confidence that they're working with a reputable, professional contractor.

BALI Accreditation Guarantees:

  • Adherence to industry best practices
  • Qualified, trained operators
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Professional business standards
  • Customer protection and support
